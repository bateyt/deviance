Deviance Patterns of Sociology Students

Deviant Activities Inventory

Students completed this short questionaire related to deviant behavior. To avoid personally identifiable information, submissions were anonymous, and the only demographic variable collected was sex.

Twenty-one students completed the survey. Over two-thirds of the respondents were female. Since females are over-represented, the figures below present data as relative proportions.
